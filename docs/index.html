First Light Flower Essences of NZ®

The First Light Flower Essences of New Zealand® provide a cohesive structure that draws upon Nature's healing matrix for healing outcomes. Unique to the forests, mountains, wetlands and coastlines of NZ, each plant, tree, orchid, fern ally, mountain grass, fern, flower, moss, sedge and rush is seen as a divine child of this land and comes under the protection of the Atua and the devic forces of Aotearoa. Each set or family of essences has a unique role to play in the human healing matrix to assist with restoring wellbeing at all levels – personality, chakras, energy field, DNA patterns, soul and spiritual aspects.
